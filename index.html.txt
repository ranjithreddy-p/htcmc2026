    <section id="about">
        <h2>About the Workshop</h2>
        <p>The workshop will provide a platform for academia and industry experts from across the world to present the state of the art in high throughput correlative materials characterization in terms of instrumentation and data analysis techniques. It also includes hands-on demonstration sessions on advanced characterization equipment.</p>
        <p>For the first time in India, experience it at the University of Hyderabad.</p>
